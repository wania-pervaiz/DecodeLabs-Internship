Our Story

At Pasta & Pine, we believe pasta is a creative ritual. Founded in 2026, we hand-roll our dough using organic semolina flour. By honoring traditional Italian techniques, we preserve the vibrant texture and flavor of every single batch we craft.

The Perfect Al-Dente Guide

To experience our pasta beautifully, use a 6-quart pot of heavily salted, boiling water. Drop the fresh pasta and cook for exactly 3 minutes. Patience in the final stage of finishing the pasta directly in the sauce pan is what unlocks the authentic restaurant-quality texture.
